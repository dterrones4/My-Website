Work

MotivATR

GitHub Live App

MotivATR is an app that allows you to remain accountable to a workout partner regardless of how far apart you may live. MotivATR automatically syncs with a users fitness tracker and will let the users partner know if certain fitness goals aren't met daily.

Date Night

GitHub Live App

Date Night is an app that pulls in data from a number of third party sites to show users things to do in there area. The list includes live events, restaurants, as well as other miscellaneous attractions like museums or amusement parks. To get started you simply enter in your address and Date Night will take care of the rest.

Game of Thrones Quiz

This Quiz app was one of my first projects when I started coding. It's a simple 10 question quiz to test all your Game of Thrones knowledge. It isn't the most impressive app but it marks the beginning of my coding journey.
